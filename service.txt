<h2>Service Opportunities </h2>
<p> Key service opportunities can be easily found depending on the sources. Certain clubs around campus make it easy to do community service; for example, Sending out Love club makes cards and quilt for children and elders and CSF gives community service opportunities out every other week. </p>

<h2> What are the requirements?</h2>
<ul> 
<li> Bring a community service sheet to be signed by the organizers.</li>
<li>Be a high schooler that is conscientous of the 40 hour graduation requirement and one that cares about the community. </li>
<li> Utilize resources online to find more opportunities. Even check other local high school websites. Often times they include other nearby activities. </li>
</ul>
